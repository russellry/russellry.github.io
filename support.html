Before You Email

To help me assist you as quickly as possible, please include (where relevant):

  • App name & version (see settings → about)
  • Device model & iOS version
  • Steps to reproduce the issue (screenshots or screen-recordings are gold ✨)
  • Your App Store purchase receipt (for billing/refund questions)

Frequently Asked Questions

🛠 Bug Reports
I love squashing bugs. Send the steps and I'll aim to patch them in the next update.

💳 Billing & Subscriptions
All payments are handled securely by Apple. For refunds, start with Apple's refund request page. If you get stuck, email me and I'll guide you.

🔐 Privacy Questions
See the Privacy Policy. Still unsure? Drop me a line.

🌟 Feature Requests
I'm always collecting ideas. Tell me why the feature matters to you (use-case, pain point) and it'll go straight to the roadmap.

Quick Troubleshooting

  • Force-quit & relaunch the app.
  • Ensure you're on the latest version from the App Store.
  • Restart your device to clear cached issues.
  • Still broken? Email me with the details above.
